# Modern Town

Modern town is a 2D top-down grid based game made with the Unity engine. The player looks down upon their town, and guides the lives of their residents. The town is made up to 200 characters, formed into family units and living in different residences across the landscape.

Members of the town carry out their daily lives - they shower, cook, sleep, and socialize. They get jobs, go to work, attend school, and participate in social and calendar events.

The game is heavily inspired by The Sims 3 mixed with Rimworld.

The aim is to create a game that captures the basic joys of the Sims 3 style open world modern life game, but adds more management depth, better simulation, and lots of content without lots of micro-transactions!
